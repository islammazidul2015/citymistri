Anatomy of a Thai Glass Sliding Door: Apnar Door Kivabe Kaj Kore?

Repair cost aur problem bojhar age, apnake jante hobe ekta Thai glass door ba window ashole kivabe toiri hoy. Ekta standard sliding door-e kichu main components thake:

  • Aluminum Profile (Frame): Eta door er skeleton. Kai, Chung Hua, ba local brand er aluminum diye eta toiri hoy.
  • Glass Panel: 5mm, 6mm ba office er jonno 10mm tempered glass use kora hoy.
  • Sliding Roller (Wheel): Door er nicher dike thake, jar upor base kore vhari glass door-ta track er upor move kore.
  • Bottom Track: Jei aluminum channel er vitor diye wheel gulo run kore.
  • Star Lock / Push Lock: Door ke lock korar mechanism.
  • Rubber Seal (Gasket): Glass ke frame er sathe tight kore dhore rakhe aur baire theke pani ba shobdo ashte dey na.

Problem A: Jammed Sliding Door (Wheel Nosto Hoye Jawa)

Sobcheye beshi je problem-ta manush face kore seta holo—door ba window tene kholte onek shokti lage, atke atke jay, ba jore shobdo hoy.

Keno hoy? Dhaka shohore prochur dhula-bali (dust). Ei dust, chul (hair), aur moila sliding track er vitor jome jay. Jokhon door open-close kora hoy, tokhon ei moila gulo wheel er bearing er sathe pechiye jay. Ek shomoy wheel ta ar ghurte pare na, jam hoye jay. Tokhon jor kore tene khulte gele wheel er nylon ba plastic body bhenge jay ba khoy hoye jay.

Problem B: Broken or Faulty Locks (Lock Kaj Na Kora)

Onek shomoy dekha jay lock er chabi ghurche na, ba vitor theke push korle lock atke jacche na.

Keno hoy? Lock er vitor choto spring thake. Door jodi ektu niche neme jay (wheel khoy howar karone), tokhon lock er hook thikmoto frame er catch-e dhuke na. Erpor jor kore lock korte gele vitorer spring ba mechanism venge jay. Er jonno pray shomoy Thai glass lock change korar dorkar hoy.

Problem C: Slamming Glass Doors (Door Closer & Floor Spring Issue)

Office ba dokan-e je push/pull heavy glass door gulo thake, shegulo chere dile onek shomoy dharam kore obosshayo jore lege jay. Eta khub e dangerous, karon glass venge accident hote pare.

Keno hoy? Ei door gulor nache ba upore 'Floor Spring' ba 'Door Closer' namer ekta machine thake, jar vitore hydraulic oil thake. Use korte korte ba purono hoye gele oil leak kore. Oil ber hoye gele door er speed control ar thake na.

Problem D: Water Leakage & Rattling Noise (Rubber Seal Problem)

Borshakale (Monsoon season) janalar pas diye pani vitor ashe, ba jhore batashe janala gulo jhon-jhon kore shobdo kore.

Keno hoy? Frame er charpashe je rubber gasket thake, sheta rod-brishti te shukhiye hard hoye jay aur chhire jay. Fole glass frame er vitore loose hoye jay.

Problem E: Misaligned Aluminum Frames

Khub low-quality aluminum use korle ba properly install na korle, kichudin por frame ektu beke jay (bend hoye jay). Fole door thikmoto bondho hoy na, faka theke jay. Ei faka diye masha (mosquitoes) easily vitor dhuke pore. Ei jonno amader Window Mosquito Net installation service ekhon khub popular.

DIY (Do It Yourself): Kivabe Nije Sliding Door Maintenance Korben?

Jodi apnar sliding door ekhono jam na hoye thake, tobe ei choto 3-ti tips follow korle apnar door er life 3x bere jabe:

  • Vacuum the Tracks: Soptaha e ontoto ekdin apnar vacuum cleaner ba ekta narrow brush diye sliding track er vitorer dhula-bali porishkar korun. Dust chara wheel koshonoi nosto hoy na.
  • Do NOT Use Mustard Oil or Grease: Ekti mukhoshthho vul koren onekei, door atke gele track er vitor shorishar tel (mustard oil), narikel tel ba grease diye den. Eta big mistake! Tel-er karone baire pressure theke dhula eshe koda (mud) toiri hoy, jeta wheel ke permanently jam kore dey. Use korle shudhu Dry Silicone Spray ba WD-40 Specialist Dirt & Dust Resistant spray use korben.
  • Handle with Care: Tene kholar shomoy halka bhabe push korben. Glass onek vhari material, jore push korle wheel er upor extreme pressure pore.
