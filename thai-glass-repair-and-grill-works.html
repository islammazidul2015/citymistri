FAQ

What Type of Material will be used?

We use genuine Kai/Chung Hua aluminum profiles, premium Max/EDF for folding doors, and original Kinglong fittings. For grill work, we use 100% rust-proof stainless steel.

How long will it take to provide service after confirmation?

For lock and wheel repairs, we provide same-day or next-day service. For new installations or partitions, it typically takes 2 to 3 working days depending on the project scale.

For mosquito net what material will be used? is magic net?

Yes! We offer the drill-free Velcro Magic Net (fiberglass mesh) which is very popular. We also provide heavy-duty Aluminum Sliding Nets for a permanent setup.

Are the prices fixed?

The prices listed are standard starting rates. The exact final price depends on your specific measurements, material thickness (e.g., 5mm vs 10mm glass), and customizations. Our mistri will quote the exact amount after inspection.

Can I change the order or specification?

Yes, you can easily change the order or specifications before the work starts. However, once the materials are cut and production begins, specifications cannot be altered without an additional charge.
