Top Reasons Why Thai Glass Locks Fail

Our expert team in Dhaka handles 10 to 15 lock replacement services daily. Based on our extensive experience, Thai glass locks typically fail due to four main reasons:

  1. Broken Wheels (Damaged Rollers): This is the biggest culprit! If the bottom wheels (rollers) of the sliding door become worn out or damaged, the entire door drops down. Consequently, the lock's hook no longer aligns with the hole in the frame. Attempting to force the lock in this state will break it.
  2. Dust and Dirt Accumulation: Dhaka city naturally has a lot of dust. Over time, this dust penetrates the lock mechanism, jamming the internal springs and pins.
  3. Cheap Materials (Local Locks): Local locks purchased for ৳100-৳150 from nearby hardware stores use low-quality plastic and inferior iron. These locks tend to bend or rust within just 2-3 months of usage.
  4. Rough Usage: Doors in commercial spaces or busy offices are often pushed or pulled very aggressively, which rapidly damages the lock's internal mechanism.

Types of Thai Glass Locks Available in BD

Various types of locks are available in the market. It is crucial to choose the right lock according to your door's profile and design:

  • Star Lock (Sliding Door Lock): This is the most common sliding door lock. It features a hook that catches onto the frame when the button is pressed or the key is turned. Original Kinglong star locks offer excellent durability.
  • Push Lock (Window Lock): Primarily used for sliding windows. Pushing the cylinder inward locks it, and pressing a button pops it open.
  • Crescent Lock: A half-round lock resembling a crescent moon that is operated by turning. These are less common in modern residential setups today.
  • Double Glass Lock: A specialized center-locking mechanism used where two sliding glass panels meet in the middle.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How long does it take to change a lock?

Typically, it takes an expert mistri about 15 to 20 minutes to completely replace a sliding door or window lock, provided there are no severe alignment issues with the frame.

Can I change the lock myself (DIY)?

If you have the proper tools (a good screwdriver) and prior DIY experience, you can try. However, it is very common for unpracticed hands to slip, driving the screwdriver into the glass and causing it to shatter. For a small fee of ৳300-৳400, it is highly recommended to hire a professional.

How can I identify an Original Kinglong lock?

The original lock features the 'Kinglong' logo perfectly and deeply engraved into the metal. The overall weight of the lock is noticeably heavier than cheap local plastics, and the keys operate exceptionally smoothly. Our mistris will show you the packaging before installation.

I lost my lock key, do I have to replace the entire lock?

Yes. Thai glass sliding locks do not have a master key system, nor is it practical to forge a new key for them. For your own security, the entire lock cylinder and casing must be replaced.
