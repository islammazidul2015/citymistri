Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which net is better for my home?

If your windows have Thai glass and you want a smart, long-lasting solution, the Thai Sliding Net is the best choice. However, if you are on a budget or live in a rented house, the Velcro Strap Net is a highly cost-effective and removable option.

Can these nets be removed for cleaning?

Yes, absolutely! The Velcro strap net can be easily pulled off the magic tape and washed. The Thai sliding nets can also be lifted off the track for easy cleaning.

How long does installation take in Dhaka?

After you confirm the order and we take precise measurements, we complete the fitting directly at your home within just 1-2 days. The process is fast and smooth!
