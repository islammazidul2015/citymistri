Mosquito Net Price in Bangladesh (2026)

Mosquito net price in Bangladesh depends on material quality, installation method and window size. Generally Velcro mosquito net price starts from around ৳55 per square foot while Thai aluminum sliding mosquito nets can cost between ৳370 and ৳480 per square foot.

For rented houses Velcro fiber mosquito net is a popular choice because it is removable and easy to clean. For permanent apartments many homeowners prefer aluminum frame sliding mosquito nets because they are durable and rat proof.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the main difference between Velcro Strap (Magic Net) and Thai Sliding Net?

Velcro Strap: This is usually attached to the window grill using magic tape. It is very easy to install and remove, and it is highly cost-effective.

Thai Sliding Net: This runs on an aluminum frame with a sliding system. It looks exactly like original Thai glass, feels premium, and is highly durable.

2. Which one would be better for my home's windows?

If your windows have Thai glass and you want a smart, long-lasting solution, the Thai Sliding Net is the best choice. However, if you are on a budget and want something temporary or easily removable attached to the iron grill, you can install the Velcro Strap Net. We provide perfect fitting for both types.

3. Can these nets be removed for cleaning?

Yes, absolutely! The Velcro strap net can be easily pulled off the magic tape, washed, and reattached. The Thai sliding nets can also be easily taken off the track along with the frame for washing and set back. Our premium fiberglass mesh generally catches less dirt.

4. How long does it take to install these nets in Dhaka or Mirpur?

After you confirm the order, our expert team will visit your home to take precise measurements. Then, within just 1-2 days, we come directly to your home and complete the fitting. There is no extra hassle, the entire process is very fast and smooth!
