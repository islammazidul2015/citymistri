1. Thai Aluminum Frame Net (Sliding)

This is a premium and permanent solution. It looks exactly like your Thai Glass window shutter but has a net instead of glass. We use Star/Kai Aluminum and SS (Stainless Steel) Net.

  • ✅ Premium Look: বাসার ইন্টেরিয়র নষ্ট করে না।

2. Velcro Fiber Net (Magic Mesh)

This is the most popular budget-friendly option. It consists of a high-quality Fiber Glass Mesh attached to the window frame using Velcro Tape (Hook & Loop system).

Mosquito Net Price in Bangladesh (2026)

Mosquito net price in Bangladesh depends on material quality, installation method and window size. Generally Velcro mosquito net price starts from around ৳55 per square foot while Thai aluminum sliding mosquito nets can cost between ৳370 and ৳480 per square foot.

For rented houses Velcro fiber mosquito net is a popular choice because it is removable and easy to clean. For permanent apartments many homeowners prefer aluminum frame sliding mosquito nets because they are durable and rat proof.
