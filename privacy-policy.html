Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Last Updated: April 2026

At City Mistri ("we", "our", or "us"), accessible from www.citymistri.xyz, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by City Mistri and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a service or contact us for an estimate, we may ask you to provide certain personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Details: Name, phone number, and email address.
  • Service Location: Your home or office address (specifically within Dhaka) required for our technicians to provide on-site services.
  • Communication Data: Details of the problem (e.g., photos of broken glass, window sizes) sent via WhatsApp or Messenger for free estimates.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our home repair and interior services.
  • Provide accurate pricing estimates based on your shared photos and location.
  • Communicate with you to schedule technician visits and send service updates.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website's user experience.

3. Data Protection and Sharing

We highly respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

Your address and phone number are only shared securely with our verified, internal expert technicians strictly for the purpose of visiting your location to complete the requested service.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Like any other professional website, City Mistri uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site, which helps us optimize our website content and provide better services.

5. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. City Mistri does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.

6. Consent

By using our website and contacting us for services, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
